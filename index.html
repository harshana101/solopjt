Our History

Expository paragraphs are sections of writing that are used to illustrate the nature of a document or text. These expository paragraphs allow the writer to demonstrate his or her writing style, language use, and method of organizing ideas.

Expository paragraphs come in many forms, and can be descriptive, narrative, argumentative, or comparative. These paragraphs allow the reader to gain some insight into the writer's writing skills and method of expressing ideas. Creating expository paragraphs requires a good understanding and a deep knowledge of the language.
